Welcome to G.L. Bajaj Institute Of Technology & Management, Greater Noida

"A magnet for leaders and inspirational figures"

GL Bajaj is an institute in Uttar Pradesh, India that aims to serve society through social initiatives. The institute's Rotaract Club has a motto of "Livelihood Enhancers Aspiring Development (L.E.A.D)". The club's activities include professional development, leadership development, and community service.

G.L. BAJAJ is dedicated to providing quality education that prepares students for success in their chosen careers. Our faculty is committed to fostering an inclusive and challenging academic environment.

With a diverse range of programs and extracurricular activities, students can explore their interests and develop skills that are vital in today's global marketplace.
